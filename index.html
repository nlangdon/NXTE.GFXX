ABOUT

ME

I’m Nate! A budding graduate from Niagara College’s Graphic Design Program. I am a Freelance Designer, located in Ontario, Canada. I specialize in Motion Design, as well as Branding and Video Editing. Prior to studying graphic design, I graduated Niagara College’s Pre-Health Science program. Right now, I am currently working as a freelance graphic & motion designer and am ready to put my skills to use and learn new things.
